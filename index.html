About Me

I am a structural engineering researcher with expertise in bridge scour hydraulics, soil–structure interaction, structural response of shallow foundations, experimental mechanics, and finite element modelling.

My recent research focuses on bridge scour and the structural response of FlexiArch bridge systems to foundation undermining, combining hydraulics, image-based monitoring, and structural analysis to investigate settlement–scour interaction mechanisms.

My background includes research and teaching roles at Queen's University Belfast, the University of Peradeniya, and Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT), with experience in undergraduate teaching, engineering analysis, and applied research.

My long-term research interests include resilient infrastructure systems, advanced structural monitoring, computational mechanics, and multidisciplinary engineering research related to future habitat systems and human exploration technologies.
